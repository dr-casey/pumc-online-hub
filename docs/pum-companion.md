# PUM Companion

**PUM Companion** is a digital app and system for creating and playing your own adventures—solo or with friends. Inspired by tools like the Mythic GM Emulator, PUM blends improvisational storytelling, random prompts, and RPG mechanics for a unique experience every time.

## Key Features

- **Solo and GM-less Play:** Designed for solo adventurers and groups without a game master
- **Random Prompts & Oracles:** Get inspiration, resolve questions, and add twists with a tap
- **Plot Track & Nodes:** Visualize your story’s structure and manage key events, mysteries, and discoveries
- **Log Panel:** Chronicle every event, twist, and outcome in a living journal
- **Entities Overview:** Manage all your characters, NPCs, and important items
- **Cloud Backup & Cross-Device Play:** Save your games to the cloud and continue your adventure on any device
- **Export & Import:** Share your stories or move them between devices easily
- **Customizable:** Play any genre, use your own prompts, and tailor the experience to your style
- **Offline Friendly:** Play anywhere, anytime—no internet required for core features
