# Entities Overview

The Entities Overview is your command center for all the important characters, NPCs, and items in your adventure.

## What You Can Do
- **View Entities:** See a list of all major characters, NPCs, and key items in your story.
- **Add & Edit:** Create new entities or update details for existing ones. Add names, descriptions, relationships, and notes.
- **Organize:** Group entities by type (e.g., heroes, villains, artifacts) for easy navigation.
- **Quick Access:** Jump directly to an entity to review or update their information during play.

## Tips
- Keep entity details up to date as your story evolves.
- Use notes to track secrets, motivations, or hidden connections.
- Entities are always saved automatically and can be edited at any time.
